The problem

Power Progressives need a way to get their friends and groups of friends to vote

What we're up against

What gets exploited:

  • Apathy
  • manufactured cynicism
  • micro-targeted disinformation
  • voter suppression

What does it do:

  • Lowers turnout
  • skews elections toward the already powerful

Mission

Help Power Progressives help their friends vote via a personal productivity app using a task list tied to the electoral calendar

How we do it

We make it easy by providing a brief simple daily task to:

  • create
  • curate
  • update
...a contact list of friends, neighbors, and family who should vote.

Voting is solitary, requires knowledge and action. We want to bring people together to get it done.
